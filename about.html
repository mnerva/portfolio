MONA NERVA

Software developer

My name is Mona Nerva, and I am an 18-year-old software development student at Tartu Vocational College. I chose this path not only because of my interest in programming but also for the future flexibility it offers, such as the ability to work remotely. Work-life balance is very important to me, and I’m thrilled with the decision I’ve made, encouraged by many friends who are developers. I enjoy continuously learning and evolving in my field.

In my spare time, I enjoy reading books, playing music, engaging in charity work and staying active through sports. Music has been a significant part of my life - I studied piano for eight years in music school, and I’ve also taught myself guitar and have been singing my entire life.
